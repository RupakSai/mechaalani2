about us With a streamlined approach to our offerings, we make it easy for you to choose the best for your needs. Typically, our products find their way to pizza and Italian restaurants, with our dedicated trucks ensuring daily deliveries. Moreover, we curate premium selections from Europe and around the world, adding an extra layer of diversity and excellence to our offerings.

Nestled in the heart of our community since 2001, our company is more than just a supplier; we're a trusted partner in your culinary journey. With a passion for quality and a dedication to service, we bring the world's finest meats to your doorstep. From succulent ham and tender pork to exotic delicacies sourced from distant lands, each product tells a story of flavor and tradition. We deliver them with care and affection through our own trucks.

But our story doesn't end with the meat; it begins with you.

We understand the heartbeat of businesses like yours and strive to be the reliable backbone of your operations. Whether you're a bustling restaurant or a thriving grocery chain, we're here to ensure that your customers experience nothing short of excellence.
Our journey is one of growth, built on strong relationships and a shared love for great food. We take pride in every cut we deliver, knowing that it contributes to the success of your business. As we look to the future, we remain committed to providing you with the highest quality meats, sourced responsibly and delivered with care. Join us as we continue to shape the landscape of the meat industry, one partnership at a time. Together, let's savor the taste of success, bite by delicious bite.
